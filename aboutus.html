ABOUT US

Welcome to the Indian Spearhead Division (ISD), we are an Indian Arma 3 MilSim community. We are a semi-casual unit dedicated to having fun rather than achieving 100% realism. We hope to create a welcoming, non-toxic, and friendly community of users who enjoy playing the game together.

ISD is the only public Indian Arma 3 Unit currently operating and was created to provide a space for players to play India-centric missions based on the Indian armed forces. It was created out of a need for missions at times that are comfortable for Indian players. Our missions are mainly fictional.
We carry out many mission types with members learning new skills every mission. We do not exclusively look for experienced players, but welcome everybody. If you are interested in joining the unit, please visit
